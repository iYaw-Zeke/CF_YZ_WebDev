Most Frequenty Asked Questions

Why did you decide to become a Web Developer?

Ideas are generated from within, that is why the world will never run out of ideas. My decision to become a developer was generated from the passion to help others and myself create something unique.

What is your design process?

The priority is meeting the customers primary needs, therefore clients are involved in all stages from concept formulation to project design and development.

- The first step begins with a meeting with clients to define problem

- Followed by conceptual/avatar design

- Project development

- Final design

- 2X free adjustments/modifications

Who are your clients?

-Any person who wishes to exhibit an idea or bring to bring a perceived idea into reality and to the rest of the world.

-Also companies, cooperate institutions, Non Profit Organizations and Volunteers.

What is your average project turnaround?

With all conditions satisfied, average turnaround is 21days. What are the conditions and mode of payment ? Clients are required to sign flexible contract of engagement after which 20% of charge is required to be paid.

How secure is data confidentiality?

Also included in the contract is data confidentiality and copyright agreement.

My page does not display properly.

Make sure you have the latest version of your web browser installed and good internet connection.

How can I retrieve my password or username?

Simply fill out the password retrieval form and a message will be sent to you via email with instructions to retrieve password.

Webpages take too long to load.

Check your internet connection or close some of opens tabs on your browser. Also make sure you have the latest version of your web browser installed
